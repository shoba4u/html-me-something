"The Willis Tower, built as and still commonly referred to as Sears Tower, is a 109-story, skyscraper in Chicago, Illinois, United States. At completion in 1973 it surpasssed the World Trade Center towers in New York to become the tallest building in the world, a title it held for nearly 25 years.
